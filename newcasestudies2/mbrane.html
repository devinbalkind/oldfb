Using the Web as a Filter for Lead Generation


Developed for B2B marketing and lead generation, the Mbrane web site has two main goals: 1) Educate businesses as to the real benefits that mobility brings to the enterprise. The site accomplished this through four animated sections that illustrate Dream, Reality, Answers and Rewards. 2) Generate leads for its mobile enterprise software infrastructure solution. Frankfurt Balkind also created the Mbrane name (previously Centura software), identity and marketing.
