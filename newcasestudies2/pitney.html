Moving from Product Silos to a Solution Brand


Pitney Bowes prior site was focused on business units, making it difficult for users to find the information they needed. With a new customer-centric strategy, users can quickly access electronic and paper-based mail, messaging and document solutions, products and services. The site broadens understanding and expands the visitor's perception of Pitney Bowes as a unified global brand. Frankfurt Balkind created the site strategy, information architecture, user interface, graphic design, and new content areas and editorial. BroadVision is the backend application platform.
