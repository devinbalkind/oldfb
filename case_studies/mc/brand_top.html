How do you build a company's brand by turning it into an experience?

You start by defining it in a way that's clear and compelling to its audiences. In MasterCard's case it's people all over the world.

To reach them, we created a 2000 sq. ft. environment with interactive kiosks, projection screens, video, and even a "wishing well," where you toss in a coin and it turns into the MasterCard logo.
