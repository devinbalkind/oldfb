Brand Experience   Corporate Communications   Chip Launch

Frankfurt Balkind's assignment was to develop the strategy and the tools for launching a new category for MasterCard International - the Chip initiative.

Our creative solution positions MasterCard as providing the complete solution for businesses to offer the payment mechanism of the future today. The technical compatability for future technical developments as well as current payment mechanisms reinforces MasterCard's leadership position.

The campaign includes a brochure and a multimedia presentation which illustrate the urgency of making a business decision to adapt this technology now. The brochure provides an overview of the chip category offerings. And the multimedia presentation provides an in-depth perspective of the endless possibilities available to grow business with this new platform.
