Communicating a New Direction

Background

EG&G is a Fortune 200 science and technology company, who has moved from a public-sector focus into the commercial marketplace over the past five years. Since 1992 we've been helping EG&Gs management communicate its vision and strategies to key audiences.

Annual Reports

The 92 annual communicated the global relevance of EG&G products

and services; The 93 annual described how EG&G's integration of divisional expertise has resulted in product innovations; The 94 annual introduced a restructuring program, outlining short- and long-term strategies and establishing criteria for success in key commercial industries; The 95 provided a "progress report;" The 96 reported on additional progress, and for specific initiatives, led readers to EG&Gs new Web site for more in-depth information.

Results

The brochure met managements objective, has been widely well-received, and we have been commissioned to work on a range of communications materials.
