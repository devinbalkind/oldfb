Creating and Promoting
a New Financial Product

Background

When Kidder, Peabody launched its new Investor Statement directed to its wealthier customers, it was an integral part of the company's positioning strategy as a leading edge financial services company.

Statement Design

The statement is directed to investors of more than $500,000. We were instrumental in determining its name and look as well as its content and information flow.

Promotional Campaign

A brochure dedicated to investors was created explaining statement changes, discussing benefits and describing how best to use the information provided.

Also created were promotional materials including posters, an internal brochure and an internal "squawk-box" advertising campaign built around the theme of "great statements," used to gain support among Kidder Peabody brokers and their assistants.
