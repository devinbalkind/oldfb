Integrating the Annual Message

Background

More than 90% of The Limited's employees are shareholders, so their annual report and annual meetings are primarily directed to that audience, as their main goal is to drive their culture and become more customer-focused.

Along with creating the communications strategy behind their annual reports for the past seven years, we helped stage their meetings, attended by more than 8,000 associates (what they call their employees) and security analysts. The theme of the report and the meeting were closely tied together, and we extended the key message into music, multimedia shows, "premiums," and the three-dimensional space which houses the meetings.
