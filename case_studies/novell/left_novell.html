New Positioning and Identity for the Network Leader

Background

Novell is the leading developer of network operating systems, holding 60% of the market worldwide. With computer networks growing from one hundred million to one billion users in the next five years, Novell wanted to make networking understandable to the non-technology audience.

We developed a new corporate positioning and an identity that communicated, in a friendly, easily understandable fashion, the idea of connecting people with people and the information they need.

The identity is the platform for Novell's evolving integrated communications program.

Annual Reports

From 1994-1996, we have created annual reports for Novell that clarifies who they are and where they are going as a networking leader in a rapidly changing technological environment.
