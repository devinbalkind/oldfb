Creating an Identity and Launching A New Retail Environment

Background

When Sony opened its first retail store and interactive entertainment center in America, located on Madison Avenue, and the entertainment giant needed a vibrant, distinctive identity and advertising campaign that would introduce and attract the public, as well as project a new kind of experience that only Sony could deliver.

Identity

A major goal of the new identity was to bring together Sonys hardware and software (entertainment) properties under a single umbrella. We created an identity that represented breaking out of traditional boundaries a swirl of rainbow colored circles-within-circles that seem to pulse with electronic movement to be used on all media forms from shopping bags and stationery to advertising. The new identity was based upon existing Sony corporate and product identities so that, while completely new, it naturally became part of the Sony family. The Sony Plaza retail identity has now become that divisions identity as well.

Advertising

The image campaign set out to let shoppers and sightseers know that this was the place they could "touch" Sony with their senses. Inside Sony Plaza theyd be able to Hear the latest Sony music releases on Sony Walkmans, See movie videos on Sony VCRs and Feel the excitement of actual movie sets. For the launch, outdoor was the medium of choice and the very graphic campaign could be seen on buses, kiosks and bus shelters all over Manhattan.
