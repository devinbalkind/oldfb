Creating a Web Site for an Interactive Festival

Background

The innovative Multimedia in the Global Interactive Community (i-Magic) Festival is an event that was founded by members of New York City's Interactive Community, commonly referred to as "Silicon Alley," with the goal of encouraging and rewarding high quality interactive programming. The focus is on interactive innovation, creativity and excellence and the promotion of multimedia to the general public. The i-Magic Festival culminates in an Awards Night announcing winners for excellence and achievement in various categories.

We were asked to design and construct a web site and information resource center for members of the global interactive community to access information about the festival and interact with industry colleagues and luminaries. Since the festival is based in New York City, we designed the web site to reflect the gritty aspects of the city giving it a distinct personality in a virtual setting.

Results
The site has been extremely successful in appealing to the creative and technology industries, generating word-of-mouth and many more entries than expected in the awards show.
