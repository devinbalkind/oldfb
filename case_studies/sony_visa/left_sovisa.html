Announcing a Major Alliance

To facilitate the sale of products through its Sony Station web site, Sony joined forces with Visa, a leader in the development of secure transaction technologies for the internet.

Sony asked us to create advertising that would announce the alliance to the financial community timed to the joint Sony/Visa press conference. The message highlighted the existing strength of the individual brand names, which gave a sense of the potential online success that Sony and Visa would have together.We were, subsequently, asked to create the web site itself, TheStation@Sony.com.
