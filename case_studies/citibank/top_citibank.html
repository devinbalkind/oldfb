An Identity for a Multi-Market, Multi-Product Organization

When Citibank Global Asset Management was formed during 1992, it brought together more than 800 investment professionals and staff members in 27 investment centers around the world. The decision created a multi-market, multi-product global organization that currently manages more than $73 billion in client assets. It also created the need for a consistent, recognizable and unified corporate identity that would effectively communicate its strengths to its many audiences in local markets and around the world.

The identity we created was designed to convey a local/global mix with a strong and unique visual image. It communicates a network of professionals with a presence in nearly all major and developing markets, and works well within Citicorp/Citibanks own design standards, enhancing rather than supplanting them.
