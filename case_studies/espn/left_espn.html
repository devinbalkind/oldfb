An Identity for a Network

ESPN's identity system was designed to be representative of the cable network's programming and appealing to its predominantly male audience. Utilizing an ESPN logotype as symbol, we adopted red as the network color and gave the letters an active look cut through by a "speed line." This was enhanced by the tie-line, "The Total Sports Network."

Results
Completed about 12 years ago, the ESPN identity system has stood up to every test an identity should meet it has visibility on small items such as microphones as well as large signage, works in film animation, for advertising usage, and upon cameras, trucks and vehicles, and continues to be at the core of new product offerings — in effect, all the arduous graphic rigors inherent to a sports and cable network.
