Adding local flavor to a national brand...

Early in the fall of 1995 national casino operator Harrah's unveiled a new casino in the city of soulful sin, New Orleans. Our task was to create a symbol relevent to both the area and the facility that would complement the existing Harrah's identity.

The new symbol, a unique rendering of a "jester" character common in the folk-art of the area, visually connotes both New Orleans and gaming at a glance. The masked fool, wearing a tricorn hat adorned with card suits and stars, is juxtaposed with the original "Harrah's" corporate logo. It now inhabits the signs, shopping bags, brochures, pins, and other information vehicles and marketing devices of the complex.
