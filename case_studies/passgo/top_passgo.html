Re-launching a company and its product offering.

PassGo, formerly CKS, is a leading enterprise management software developer, providing access and control solutions for Global 2000 companies. Its software enables businesses to easily and securely provide employees access to the information and tools they need to work productively, while allowing IT administrators to manage corporate resources from a single point of control.

Experiencing moderate success and growth over 15 years, PassGo needed to redefine its position in the marketplace and raise its visibility, especially in the U.S., in preparation for an IPO within 12 months. FB created a core positioning of "Access and Control Simplified," and developed a new name, identity and integrated communications program.

Naming

The name PassGo was chosen for both the company and product, not only to reflect the energetic, entrepreneurial nature of the company, but to capture the essential benefit of its software: to provide employees secure access to corporate resources (pass) and enable them to quickly and easily be productive in their work (go).

Identity

The new identity uses color and symbols to convey two core elements, "access" and "control," of the positioning statement. The bold graphic approach and the easily grasped nature of the overall mark speak to the third element of its positioning, "simplified", thereby communicating the efficiency of the product.

Brochure

The first extension of the new identity, the simple cover treatment, focuses on the new name, symbol and positioning. The first four spreads amplify the messages of the cover in an advertising format, breaking down the core positioning and describing the market opportunity. Subsequent spreads relate the company's history of successfully meeting the demands of Fortune 2000 firms across the globe, provide details on the features of its software solutions and speak to its commitment to customers to ensure their success.

Website

As part of the integrated branding effort, we redesigned the corporate Website to redefine the product offering, explain its comprehensive solutions, provide case studies and increase functionality.
