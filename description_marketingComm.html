Marketing Communications

Our world-renowned excellence in all forms of design and our highly developed strategic and branding capabilities produce work with unique marketing power.

This work addresses four key targets:
For your external corporate audiences - the financial community, the media, potential employees and partners-we develop everything from annual reports, corporate brochures, and videos, to recruitment programs, shareholder meetings, and other forms of environmental design. For your internal corporate audiences, we create newsletters, promotional campaigns, and training programs to inform and motivate, as well as to stimulate corporate culture. For your business customers, we provide materials that support the entire B2B marketing cycle. Product brochures, videos, trade show and symposium design. Plus direct marketing programs that drive potential customers to everything from the first meeting to the deal closing. For consumer audiences, we produce brand image and product brochures, packaging, point-of-purchase displays, posters, catalogs, exhibits, and retail environments. The overall goal is to produce communications designed to move people from first-time awareness to long-term customer relationship.
