HILL, HOLLIDAY SIGNS DEAL TO ACQUIRE FRANKFURT BALKIND

Agency Expands Brand Identity Offerings from the New York Office;

Frankfurt Balkind Retains its Name

 

NEW YORK, July 1, 2002 . . .Hill, Holliday, Connors, Cosmopulos today expanded its creative and strategic branding disciplines out of the New York office by announcing it has reached an agreement to acquire Frankfurt Balkind, a well-established communications firm specializing in strategic positioning, brand identity, web/interactive development, marketing communications, and design. Frankfurt Balkind will function as a center of excellence in strategic branding for the entire Hill, Holliday network, as well as continue to pursue its own clients.

Financial terms were not disclosed.

Founded in 1972, Frankfurt Balkind is an award-winning communications agency that has established itself as a highly integrated, creative force with clients spanning a number of industries, including technology, financial, publishing, health and beauty, retail, and entertainment. As one of the early innovators in the area of brand integration, Frankfurt Balkind is built upon a unique model of cross-functional strategic teams including corporate, marketing, identity, advertising, and web strategists who work together to deliver strategic solutions to their clients.

They have created brand coherence across all media for an impressive group of clients including: Sony, Pitney Bowes, Knight Ridder, Schering-Plough, The Guggenheim Museum, The Getty Museum and Fordham University. Aubrey Balkind, who will retain his title of president of the Frankfurt Balkind division, adds: Hill, Holliday and Interpublic will provide us the platform to work with an important group of clients where strategic creative needs to be supported by global execution. We look forward to this next challenge in our development.

The acquisition of Frankfurt Balkind enables Hill, Hollidays New York office to complement its current services in the areas of strategy, identity and digital communications. What we are creating here is an opportunity for clients to solve more of their branding needs with a talented group of people who will actually work together, said David Wecal, President of Hill, Hollidays New York office. Clients are asking for more collaboration between these functions. Their businesses are demanding it. Seamless collaboration and creative excellence will be our hallmark.

Added David Kessler, Managing Director of Hill, Holliday/NY, This new combined organization augments a series of strategic initiatives that Hill, Holliday has made over the last year to pursue growth and expand our integrated marketing capabilities. We are building the New York office to respond to the markets needs.

Last month, the agency hired advertising veteran Norman Sherman to develop a healthcare unit for Hill, Holliday. Sherman worked for thirteen years at DMB&B, New York, most recently as EVP with responsibility for the agencys global Procter & Gamble healthcare business. And in November, Hill, Holliday added to its stable of talent with the appointment of Elizabeth DeMaso as Director of Business Development. DeMaso was previously SVP of Business Development at Deutsch, New York, where for five years she contributed to the addition of more than $800 million in agency billings.

Last month, Hill, Hollidays New York office, which already handles PricewaterhouseCoopers professional services account, added the $80 million assignment for their consulting division (now called Monday:) And last week, Hill, Holliday in Boston was appointed lead agency for CVS, Americas #1 pharmacy.

 

About Hill, Holliday

Hill, Holliday, owned by the Interpublic Group of Companies Inc. (NYSE:IPG) and headquartered in Boston with offices in New York City and San Francisco, is one of the top communications agencies in the U.S., with current billings of nearly $1 billion. In 2001, Hill, Holliday was chosen as Adweeks New England Agency of the Year, Marketing Computers National Agency of the Year and BtoB Magazines Best Full Service Agency. The agency has won numerous awards for advertising excellence and effectiveness and has among its roster of clients leading national and regional brands such as John Hancock, FleetBoston Financial, Anheuser-Busch, Red Roof Inns, PricewaterhouseCoopers, PwC Consulting, Beringer Blass Wine Estates, Minolta, Verizon Wireless, Popeyes Chicken & Biscuits, Marshalls and Dunkin Donuts.
